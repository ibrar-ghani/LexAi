# LexAI: Intelligent Legal Research Assistant

LexAI is a high-performance, browser-based research assistant specifically engineered to help legal professionals and businesses in the KSA market analyze complex contracts and legal documents. By leveraging Google's Gemini API, LexAI transforms static PDFs into interactive, actionable knowledge bases[cite: 1].

## Specialized Legal Features
- **Contract Risk Analysis**: Specifically prompted to act as a sharp legal analyst to identify "traps," one-sided clauses, and hidden obligations within legal agreements[cite: 1].
- **KSA Market Ready**: Features native Right-to-Left (RTL) support and a fully localized Arabic/English interface for seamless regional legal workflows[cite: 1].
- **Cited Evidence**: Every insight is backed by direct references to your specific contract sections, ensuring auditability and precision[cite: 1].
- **Multi-document Intelligence**: Query entire portfolios of legal documents at once to uncover inconsistencies or cross-reference obligations[cite: 1].
- **Privacy-First**: Your sensitive legal documents are processed locally or via your personal API key; your data never leaves your control[cite: 1].

## Getting Started

1. Visit the live app: [https://lex-ai-blond.vercel.app](https://lex-ai-blond.vercel.app)
2. Obtain your Google Gemini API key from [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey).
3. Paste your API key into the "Gemini API Key" section in the left panel.
4. Upload your legal PDFs and start your analysis[cite: 1]!

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3 (with Flexbox/Grid), Vanilla JavaScript[cite: 1].
- **LLM Integration**: Google Gemini API[cite: 1].
- **PDF Processing**: `pdf.js`[cite: 1].
- **Deployment**: Vercel[cite: 1].
